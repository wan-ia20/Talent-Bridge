Our Story

Built by Job Seekers, for Job Seekers

TalentBridge was founded in 2019 by a team of engineers and recruiters who were frustrated with the broken state of online hiring. Too many irrelevant listings. Too little transparency. Too much friction.

We built the platform we wished existed — one where candidates are treated as people, not resumes, and companies are evaluated on their culture as much as their perks.

Today, we serve over 2 million professionals across 80+ countries, with a mission to make meaningful employment accessible to everyone.
